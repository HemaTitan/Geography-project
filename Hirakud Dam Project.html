Hirakud Project

Hirakud Dam Project is a multipurpose scheme intended for flood control, irrigation and power generation. The dam is built across river Mahanadi at about 15 km upstream of Sambalpur town in the state of Odisha.T he aim of this project was to control flood, supply water for irrigation purpose and hydropower power genera tion. The construction of dam was started as in 1940's. The first notification for the land acquisition was issued on 13 September 1946.
