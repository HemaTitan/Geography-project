Major Multi Purpouse Project

A multipurpose project is a massive project which serves a variety of purposes like- flood control, fish breeding, irrigation, generation of electricity, soil conservation, etc.

Bhakhra Nangal Projectis India's biggest multi-purpose river valley project so far completed at a cost of Rs. 236 Cr. It consists of a straight gravity dam, 518 mts long & 226 mts high across the Sutlej at Bhakhra.
